# Story 1.6: Footer & Navigation

## Status
Approved

## Story
**As a** User,
**I want** to easily navigate the site and access contact info in the footer,
**so that** I can explore the brand efficiently and find policy information.

## Acceptance Criteria
1.  [ ] **Header (Navigation):**
    *   Transparent background, becoming solid/glassy on scroll.
    *   Logo: "Primed'Or" (Left or Center).
    *   Links: Shop, Story, Contact (Right or Split).
    *   Mobile: Hamburger Menu -> Side Drawer.
2.  [ ] **Footer:**
    *   3 Columns: Brand Summary, Quick Links (Shipping, Returns), Social Icons.
    *   Copyright Notice & "Built with Passion".
    *   Newsletter Subscription Input (Visual only for now).
3.  [ ] **Responsive:** Header collapses to Mobile Menu on small screens. Footer stacks vertically.
4.  [ ] **Style:** Consistent Fonts (Playfair for headings, Inter for links).
