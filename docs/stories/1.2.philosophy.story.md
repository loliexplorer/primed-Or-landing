# Story 1.2: Philosophy Section - "Soul of Nature"

## Status
Approved

## Story
**As a** Brand Manager,
**I want** a dedicated "Philosophy" section below the Hero,
**so that** I can communicate Primed'Or's commitment to natural, safe, and sustainable ingredients ("Soul of Nature").

## Acceptance Criteria
1.  [ ] **Layout:** 2-column layout on Desktop (Text Left, Image Right) | Stacked on Mobile.
2.  [ ] **Content:**
    *   Heading: "Triết lý của chúng tôi" (Our Philosophy) or "Soul of Nature".
    *   Body: Paragraph describing the respect for nature and safety.
    *   Visual: High-quality nature-inspired image (e.g., ingredients, candle close-up).
3.  [ ] **Animation:** Elements should fade in/slide up as the user scrolls into view (Scroll-triggered).
4.  [ ] **Design:** Usage of `--accent-sage` or Earth tones to differentiate from the dark Hero section.
5.  [ ] **Performance:** LCP not impacted (lazy load this image).
