# Story 1.4: Collections Showcase

## Status
Approved

## Story
**As a** Customer,
**I want** to see the main product categories (Collections),
**so that** I can easily navigate to the type of product I am interested in (Candles, Oils, or Diffusers).

## Acceptance Criteria
1.  [ ] **Layout:** 3-Column Grid (Desktop) | Stacked (Mobile).
2.  [ ] **Content:** 3 Core Collections:
    *   **Artisan Candles** (Nến Thơm Nghệ Thuật).
    *   **Pure Essential Oils** (Tinh Dầu Nguyên Bản).
    *   **Reed Diffusers** (Bộ Khuếch Tán Tinh Tế).
3.  [ ] **Visuals:** High-quality product lifestyle shots for each category.
4.  [ ] **Interaction:**
    *   Hovering over a category image darkens/zooms basic overlay.
    *   "Explore" (Khám phá) button appears or highlights on hover.
