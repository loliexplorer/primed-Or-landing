# Story 1.3: Ingredients Section - "Earth to Soul"

## Status
Approved

## Story
**As a** Product Manager,
**I want** an interactive "Ingredients" slider,
**so that** users can explore the premium, natural raw materials (Lavender, Sandalwood, Jasmine) used in our products.

## Acceptance Criteria
1.  [ ] **Layout:** Horizontal Carousel/Slider interactive component.
2.  [ ] **Content:** At least 3 key ingredients:
    *   Lavender (Hoa Oải Hương)
    *   Sandalwood (Gỗ Đàn Hương)
    *   Jasmine (Hoa Nhài)
3.  [ ] **Card Structure:** Each slide contains a macro background image, Ingredient Name, and a "Benefit" caption.
4.  [ ] **Interaction:**
    *   **Desktop:** Hovering over a card expands it slightly or reveals more text.
    *   **Mobile:** Horizontal swipeable list.
5.  [ ] **Visual Style:** "Card-based" or "Split-pane" design. Consistent with "Earth" theme (Stone/Beige/Green colors).
